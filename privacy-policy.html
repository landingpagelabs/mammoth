Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Mammoth Projects (“Mammoth,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or submit an inquiry, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through our website, we collect the information you choose to provide, which may include your name, email address, home address, and details about your project. We may also automatically collect basic technical information — such as your browser type and approximate location — through standard web server logs.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • respond to your inquiry and discuss your project;
  • prepare for and schedule consultations;
  • send you occasional news and updates, if you have opted in;
  • operate, maintain, and improve our website and services.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us run our business — for example, the form service (Formspree) that delivers your inquiry to us — and only to the extent needed to provide that service. We may also disclose information if required to do so by law.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your Choices & Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you by contacting us using the details below. You can also unsubscribe from our updates at any time using the link included in our emails.

Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies needed for it to function properly. We do not use cookies to track you across other websites.
